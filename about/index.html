About

Historic district charm, quieter hospitality.

The current Centennial House site positions the inn around three things that matter: walkability, room character, and breakfast. That is the right foundation.

One block from Flagler College and close to downtown landmarks, the property is set up for guests who want to park, settle in, and spend the rest of the trip on foot.

Property basics

  • Adults-only stay, age 21 and over
  • Historic district location
  • Private baths in each room
  • Independent inn atmosphere, not a chain hotel
