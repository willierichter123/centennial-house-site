Good to know

Is Centennial House adults-only?

Yes. Centennial House is an adults-only property, welcoming guests 18 and older. The inn is designed for a quieter, more intimate experience.

Is breakfast included with every stay?

Yes. A full breakfast is included with every room. It's served each morning in a relaxed setting and is one of the things guests mention most often.

What are check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is from 3:00 PM to 6:00 PM. Check-out is by 11:00 AM. If you need to arrange a different arrival time, reach out ahead of your stay and we'll do our best to accommodate.

Where do guests park?

Complimentary on-site parking is available for guests. The historic district is very walkable, so most guests park once and explore on foot for the duration of their stay.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary by reservation. The details will be shown at checkout through our booking system. For questions about an existing booking, call or email us directly.

How do I make a reservation?

Reservations are made through our online booking system. You can check availability and rates at any time — just use the Book Now button anywhere on the site.
