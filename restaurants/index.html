Restaurants

Dinner planning belongs on the property site too.

The current site separates restaurants into its own page. That is smart. A couple booking a historic inn often wants to know what is close, worth reserving, and right for the tone of the trip.

What to improve is the usefulness of the recommendations. Keep the restaurant page, but make it practical: distance, atmosphere, reservation notes, and a quick sense of what each place is best for.
