Alexandrite

One of the more classic Centennial rooms, Alexandrite pairs Cordova Street views with a four-poster king bed and a more traditional historic-inn atmosphere.

Room overview

Historic character with a little more ceremony.

Restored windows overlook Cordova Street, where horse-drawn carriages pass by and the historic setting feels especially present. Alexandrite has a little more old-world personality than some of the brighter rooms.

Guests who want a king bed, a stronger sense of place, and a room that feels rooted in the house itself will likely gravitate here.
