Room overview

A celebratory room with more atmosphere.

Onyx is designed for guests who want the room itself to feel like part of the occasion. It is more dramatic than the lighter rooms, with stronger contrast and a more cocooned, evening-ready mood.

For an anniversary weekend or a stay that should feel a little more elevated, it is one of the most memorable options in the house.
