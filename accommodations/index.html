Policies

Carriage Way Centennial Policies

First-night deposit is charged at booking and the balance is processed the day before arrival. Adults-only, no smoking or vaping indoors, no pets except qualified ADA service dogs, and all rooms sleep two guests only.

Breakfast is served table side each morning from 8:30am to 10:00am. With advance notice the inn can accommodate gluten-free and vegetarian diets, but not egg-free, dairy-free, or vegan diets.

Check-in and parking registration runs from 11:00am to 5:00pm, rooms are guaranteed by 4:00pm, and after-hours arrival requires advance lockbox coordination. Onsite parking is not guaranteed.
