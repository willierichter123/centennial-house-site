A Weekend Itinerary for First-Time St. Augustine Visitors

Dec 18, 2025 Carriage Way Centennial House

Two days, one carry-on, zero stress. This is the itinerary we hand to first-time guests when they ask us how to spend a weekend — built around walkability and the things people consistently say they loved.

Friday afternoon

Check in, drop the bag, walk five minutes to the bayfront. Sit there for an hour. Have an early dinner at one of the smaller spots on Hypolita Street. Walk the lit-up plaza after — St. Augustine is one of those cities that gets prettier after dark.

Saturday morning

Breakfast at the inn, then the Castillo before the crowds. From there, work south through Aviles Street and across to the Lightner Museum. Lunch on King Street, then back here for a slow afternoon on the porch or a nap.

Saturday evening

If you’re up for it, the ghost tours are corny but fun. If you’re not, dinner at a place we’ll happily reserve for you and a walk along the bridge for the lights.

Sunday morning

Slow breakfast. Walk to a coffee place we like. If the weather is good, the trolley to the lighthouse and Anastasia is a worthwhile last stop before checkout. If not, an extra hour on the porch is also a perfectly correct answer.
