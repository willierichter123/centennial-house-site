What to Expect at Breakfast: A Morning at Centennial House

Mar 22, 2026 Carriage Way Centennial House

Breakfast here isn’t a buffet station tucked behind the elevator. It’s the part of the day we put the most thought into — and the part most guests bring up first when they’re asked what made the stay.

Mornings start quietly. Coffee is ready by seven, and the dining room opens shortly after. We rotate the menu through a small set of plates we genuinely love making, so you’ll see a different hot dish every day of a multi-night stay.

What’s actually served

Each morning includes a hot main, a fresh fruit course, a baked good (often something we made at five a.m.), local juice, and full coffee and tea service. Dietary requests are easy to accommodate — tell us at booking and we’ll plan around it.

When and where

Breakfast is served from 8:00 to 9:30 in the dining room. You don’t need to sign up or pick a slot — come down whenever feels right. Most guests linger longer than they planned.

Why guests talk about it

We think it’s less about the food (though we’re proud of the food) and more about the pacing. There’s no buffet line, no plastic creamer, no rush. It’s the kind of breakfast that resets your morning before you walk out the front door.
