Settle in, slow down, and let the stay feel distinct from the city around it.

Centennial House is for guests who want St. Augustine to feel more personal from the moment they arrive. The old city is nearby, breakfast becomes part of the pace of the morning, and the inn is meant to feel quieter than the streets outside.

Whether the plan is a romantic weekend, a celebratory few days away, or simply a slower stay with room to breathe, the goal is the same: more character, more calm, and less of the standard hotel feeling.

Breakfast In Style

Breakfast is woven into the stay, not added on afterward.

Mornings begin with a freshly prepared breakfast served in a setting that feels relaxed, intimate, and suited to the pace of an inn rather than a hotel.

It is one of the clearest ways Centennial House distinguishes itself and helps the day start with a little more ease.
